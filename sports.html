Our Mission

The India Sport Institute hyderabad is committed to the pursuit of excellence by providing world-class programs, services, and leadership to high performance athletes and coaches to enhance their ability to achieve international podium performances.


Our Vision

The India Sport Institute Hyderabad is a recognized world leader in high performance sport, resulting in more India athletes on the international podium.


Our Values


PURSUIT OF EXCELLENCE:


As experts in our field, we are dedicated to supporting athletes and coaches in their daily training environment. We ask of ourselves what athletes and coaches demand of themselves. We seek out the most effective and efficient methods to achieve organizational excellence. As a result, we are united in our purpose to help athletes and coaches achieve international podium results.


FUELING THE PASSION:


We take great pride in working at the CSIO and our role in India sport. It is our passion that holds us accountable to each other, the athletes and coaches we serve, and the system that supports us.


DRIVING INNOVATION:


We employ cutting edge practices in all that we do. We are not afraid to take risks. We are committed to sharing our knowledge – learning from what works and from what doesn’t. We continuously improve and adapt to an ever-changing landscape. We are fearless in our desire to remain ahead of the curve.


LIVING WITH INTEGRITY:

We promise to uphold the highest standards of care at all levels of the organization. We believe in treating others and each other with respect; ensuring fairness and transparency in our policies, procedures and decisions. We create a holistic work environment where people feel valued and supported.


AUTHENTIC LEADERSHIP:


In partnership with other sport leaders, we recognize our role in elevating the institution of sport. We work collaboratively with others to generate robust solutions to common problems, provide a caring environment to stimulate growth, and invest in practices that contribute to consistent and ethically-based results.


Strategic Plan

CSIO’s strategic plan was developed by conducting a thorough review of the internal and external environment, by reviewing the recommendations contained in its business plan, and through extensive discussions with staff, Board and stakeholders. An important component of the strategic planning process was renewing the CSIO’s commitment to its mission and vision and identifying and defining a core set of values to help ensure consistency in decision-making across the organization. The strategic plan is intended to inform stakeholders about what the organization will be focusing on over the next four years and articulates a set of core activities along with specific goals that the CSIO is striving to achieve over this timeframe.


CSIO Strategic Plan Overview


STRATEGIC PRIORITIES

Strategic Priority 2: Build a stronger high performance sport system in Ontario
Strategic Priority 3: Create and maintain a world-class workplace
